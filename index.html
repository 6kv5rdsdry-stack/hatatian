Our Story

A private jeweler
rooted in Nairobi.

Hatatian Jewelry was born from a singular belief: that Africa's earth holds some of the most extraordinary gemstones in the world — and that those stones deserve to be treated with integrity, artistry, and reverence from the moment they leave the ground.

"We don't sell jewelry. We create heirlooms — one stone, one person, one story at a time."

As a private jeweler operating by appointment from our Lavington Aterlier, every relationship begins with a conversation. There is no catalogue to browse, no shelf to scan. Instead, we sit with you, understand your story, and build something that will outlive the moment it was made for.

The Gemstones
Hatatian Gems

Stones with
stories older
than time.

Kenya and East Africa sit atop one of the most geologically remarkable regions on earth. The same ancient forces that shaped the Great Rift Valley also crystallised some of the world's rarest gems. We source them with care, trace them with integrity, and transform them with artistry.

Enquire About a Stone
Tsavorite Garnet
Tsavo, Kenya · Discovered 1967

The rarest of garnets — a vivid green of extraordinary saturation and brilliance. Found only in the arc stretching from Tanzania through Kenya, tsavorite is prized above emerald by those who know stones.

Rarity
Teal Sapphire
Kenya & East Africa

Where blue meets green in a single stone. Teal sapphires shift with the light — from deep ocean at dusk to bright sky at noon. No two are alike, and their character only deepens over time.

Rarity
Alexandrite
East Africa · Colour-change

One of the most coveted phenomena in gemology — alexandrite shifts from forest green in daylight to deep burgundy under incandescent light. A stone that is never the same stone twice.

Rarity
Spessartite Garnet
East Africa · Vivid Orange

A garnet of electrifying orange fire — the vivid mandarin tones of top-quality spessartite are among the most arresting colours in the natural world. East Africa yields some of the finest known specimens.

Rarity
Aquamarine
East Africa · Pale Blue

The blue of still water at first light — aquamarine carries a serene clarity that no other stone replicates. East African material is known for its exceptional transparency and gentle, unwavering colour.

Rarity
Ethical Sourcing
Fully Traced · Mine to Atelier

Every Hatatian Gems stone arrives with a clear trace of its journey — who found it, where, and how. We work only with mining partners who protect both the land and the livelihoods of local communities.

Our Promise
04
Ethics & Origin
Stewardship

We are stewards,
not just sellers.

Africa has long watched its most precious resources leave its borders with too little benefit returned to the communities that uncovered them. Hatatian Jewelry was founded to be different.

"The earth gives. We are obligated to give back with the same generosity."

We source directly. We pay fairly. We trace openly. And we tell the full story of every stone — because you deserve to know exactly what you are wearing, and who made it possible.

The Bespoke Journey
How It Works

From first word
to finished piece.

We take on a limited number of commissions each season. Every journey is unhurried, intentional, and completely personal to you.

01
Private Consultation

We meet one-on-one at our Lavington atelier on Olenguruone Avenue. You share your vision, your occasion, the story you want to carry. We listen without interruption.

02
Stone Selection

From our Hatatian Gems collection, we bring out stones selected for you. You hold them. You feel them in different lights. You choose the one that speaks to you.

03
Design & Craft

Our Nairobi artisans handcraft your piece. You receive progress updates and have final approval before completion. Nothing proceeds without your blessing.

04
The Unveiling

We present your finished jewel in a private unveiling at the atelier. You leave with the story of your piece — the stone, the hands, the earth it came from.
